With Bill Murray

Bill Murray is known for his acting roles in such classic films as "Groundhog Day", "Ghostbusters", and "Stripes". However, in recent years, Murray has become known for his awesome, enigmatic celebrity behavior. From stories on the set to sightings in Karaoke clubs and even crashing random house parties, here are some of Bill Murray encounters recently reported.

Bill Murray at the airport

I was 13 years old and with my Father at O’Hare airport when I spotted Bill Murray waiting to check into his flight. I told my Dad “Hey, that’s Bill Murray!” My Dad, never one to be star struck, answered back, “Sure looks like it”.
